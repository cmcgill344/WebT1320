Greatest Rock Albums of the 90's

Pearl Jam Ten

Ten is the debut studio album by American rock band Pearl Jam, released on August 27, 1991, through Epic Records. It is considered by many to be one of the greatest albums of all time. As of 2013, it has sold 13 milliom copies in the US.

Alice In Chains Dirt

Dirt is the second studio album by Seattle band Alice in Chains. The album is considered one fo the few mainstream albums that foucs on addiction. As of 2021, it has sold 4 million copies.

Nirvana Nevermind

Nevermind was the seccond album by rockband Nirvana. It is considered to be groundbreaking in mainstream rock. With over 30 million albums sold, it considered by many to not only be the greatest album of the 90's, but of all time.
